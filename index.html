What is
SLUSH ?

SLUSH is Europe's leading startup and technology event, which connects startups and tech talent with top-tier i nternational investors, executives, and media. In 2015, SLUSH brought together 15,000 attendees including 1,700 startups, 800 venture capital investors and 630 journalists from exactly 100 countries for the two day event held in Helsinki, Finland. People from more than half of the world's countries traveled to Helsinki, with the ambition of taking their business to the next level.

SLUSH is a non-profit event organized by a community of entrepreneurs, investors, students and festival organizers. Although SLUSH has grown from a 300-person event to become one of the leading events of its kind in the world, the philosophy behind it has remained the same: to help the next generation of great, world-conquering companies forward. Know SLUSH.

What is
GLOBAL IMPACT ACCELERATOR?

SLUSH Global Impact Accelerator (GIA) is a program created in collaboration with the Ministry for Foreign Affairs of Finland and multiple partners globally. The purpose is to create a leading global program to support startups that are looking to solve some of the most pressing global challenges and contributing to the discussion on impact investing.

SLUSH Global Impact Accelerator 2016 brings impactful entrepreneurs from emerging markets to Helsinki to accelerate their business, attract additional financing and make an even greater impact on their societies and beyond. The program includes online pre-event coaching and Helsinki boot camp program from November 22-December 2, 2016 culminating in SLUSH.

Why
SLUSH IN NEPAL?

SLUSH acts as a bridge between startups and investors, media and executives. Nepal, now more than ever, is excited about the prospects of booming business that are centered around technology. We have seen instances around the world where technology form the core of business, and successfully so. Lately, Nepal also has shown exciting prospects in this area.

We have start ups and we have established companies with innovative products that are not just nationally significant but also have international prospects. Right now, an opportunity to take these products to international investors is not just less, but nonexistent. SLUSH Nepal could be a pioneer in taking Nepalese products to an important international platform of a huge scale such as SLUSH. YoungInnovations as convener of mobile social networking hub ( www.mobilenepal.net ) is proud to bring this event in Nepal.

What are the criteria in which
THE PRODUCTS WILL BE JUDGED?
Product / Protoype

How well does the product fit the needs of the challenge it sets out to solve? How user friendly is the product? How far is the product complete in finding the solution? Is it a complete product or does it still have a long way to go?

Market potential

What is the size and demographic characteristics of the consumers? What is the target consumer and their affordability of the product? What is the competition of the product? How can your product be differentiated from others? How does the change in market influence the product? What are the plans for market changes like inflation?

Gained traction

What is the user engagement and user growth rate? What is the revenue growth for the product? Have there been contracts with larger enterprises that have shown significant value to company's product/service?

Competence of your team

Does the product have have an effective management team (where one exists) behind it? Do the people involved in the business have a diverse range of skills? Does the team have the potential to lead product’s expansion in the market?

Investment readiness

Is there clear evidence of a market need for your products or services? Is there a comprehensive business plan? Is the legal structure of the company complete? Is the company compliant with current legislation and regulatory requirements?

Impact

How much impact does the product have? How big a global challenge does it address? How efficiently can the product link challenge to its solution with the goal of creating an impact?
